Thiruvananthapuram

Thiruvananthapuram, the capital city of Kerala, is a vibrant blend of history, culture, and modern development. Known for its iconic Sri Padmanabhaswamy Temple, beautiful beaches, museums, and educational institutions, the city serves as the administrative and cultural heart of the state. With its rich heritage, lush greenery, and growing technological infrastructure, Thiruvananthapuram offers visitors a unique experience that combines tradition with progress. The city is also home to Technopark, one of India's largest IT parks, making it an important center for innovation and economic growth.
