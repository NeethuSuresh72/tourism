Palakkad

Palakkad, often referred to as the "Gateway of Kerala," is known for its vast paddy fields, rich cultural heritage, and scenic landscapes. Situated at the foothills of the Western Ghats, the district is famous for the Palakkad Gap, a natural mountain pass that connects Kerala and Tamil Nadu. Attractions such as Palakkad Fort, Malampuzha Dam, Silent Valley National Park, and Nelliyampathy Hills make it a popular destination for nature lovers and history enthusiasts.
